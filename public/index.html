LIFE CYCLE COSTS
CALCULATOR

EnPak has many important benefits surrounding fuel usage, maintenance costs, and asset life extension:

  • EnPak can significantly reduce expensive truck engine maintenance costs such as exhaust after treatment, oil changes, filter replacements (fuel, air, oil, transmission), and wear on belts/hoses.
  • EnPak runs with the truck turned off, so it can lower fuel use up to 30%.
  • Extend asset life by reducing the truck's engine idle time by up to 75%.

The results are intended to illustrate the possible fuel savings, maintenance savings, and asset life extension savings using EnPak and are based on user input. They do not constitute a proposal or a guarantee.
